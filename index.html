Year of Illinois Quantum

A community-led narrative to align, amplify, and celebrate Illinois’ quantum momentum.

Overview

2026 is shaping up to be a defining year for quantum in Illinois. Global attention is growing, driven by technological advances, investment, major conferences, and international companies establishing a presence here. That momentum creates a unique opportunity to build on our quantum community.

Across research, startups, enterprise adoption, workforce development, and global convening, an unprecedented concentration of quantum activity is taking place in the region. The Year of Illinois Quantum is a community-led narrative designed to align, amplify, and celebrate this momentum under a shared banner.

This is not a new organization, conference, or governing body. It is a unifying story that reflects what is already happening and gives the ecosystem a common language to tell it together.

Why Illinois

Illinois has quietly become one of the most complete quantum ecosystems in the world.

Illinois brings together

  • World-class academic and national lab research
  • Public-private investments and infrastructure
  • A growing base of quantum startups
  • International quantum companies planting flags
  • Strong enterprise and cloud participation
  • A dense calendar of global quantum conferences

Purpose

The Year of Illinois Quantum exists to

  • Align the ecosystem around a shared narrative
  • Increase global visibility for Illinois-based quantum work
  • Perpetuate the International Year of Quantum
  • Connect industry, academia, government, and investors
  • Attract talent, capital, and partnerships to the region
  • Create continuity across conferences and initiatives

CQE Workshop Proposals

Funding: $1,500 + matching per workshop

Due date: Rolling submissions

CQE is inviting one-page proposals for in-person or hybrid workshops that advance quantum science and engineering, build cross-sector collaboration, and strengthen the regional ecosystem.

Priority themes

  • Bridge multiple institutions and sectors
  • Facilitate lively exchange across research, deployment, and workforce development
  • Relevant to academia, national labs, and industry

Submit a proposal or contact quantum@uchicago.edu.
